About

The Pizza2 story began in 1993 in Toronto, Ontario, when a young pizza lover was eager to share their work. Pizza2 has provided delicious and exciting food concepts for a fast-paced lifestyle, including top quality pizza and much more. We want to give customers the ability to make their own pizza the way they want to. With top quality ingredients, products and services our customers are guareented to always be satisfied.
